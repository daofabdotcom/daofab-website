FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do you support fiat escrow?

Yes, we work with our partners to support fiat, digital asset or stablecoin escrow.

How do I obtain tokens, digital assets or stable coins?

You may obtain tokens, digital assets or stable coins from different on ramp providers such as a fiat to crypto exchange by exchanging fiat currency to tokens, digital assets or stable coins.

How can I utilise this service as a professional services provider (e.g. trust company, escrow agents, law firm)?

You may deploy our platform on a white label basis for your clients to interact with any blockchain (public or private) and we can support integration with different opening banking API. Speak to us today to understand our different deployment models available for professional service providers.

As a business, how do I sign-up for early access to the system?

You may sign-up for early access to the system through our Trusted Partners. If you are interested to participate in our partner testing program, speak to us today and we can connect you with one of our Trusted Partners to obtain early access to the system.

What type of use cases are you able to support?

We support a wide variety of use cases (e.g. deposit for mergers and acquisitions, trade finance, vessel sale etc) where funds are locked pending completion of certain conditions. Our partners generally use a blockchain settlement system to support real-time settlement or transactions which are traditionally not well supported by the existing payment rails.

Do I need to obtain a bank account to obtain receipt of the funds?

No, we work with our partners to enable funds to be off-ramped. In all other instances, the digital assets are secured by a smart contract on the applicable blockchain network to allow a professional service provider to provide certain services in a non-custodial manner.

How does the stablecoins / digital asset in escrow generate yield?

Parties to the escrow are able to lend the funds to Compound, a leading decentralized finance protocol.

The overall process sounds complicated, how does it work?

The application works similar to your usual bank transfer (with more customisation). Once the escrow is set-up, parties can jointly decide on any release or revert of funds. Not yet convinced? View a 1-min video of the process.
